Privacy Policy
Home Tab — New Tab Extension

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Overview
Home Tab is a minimal, privacy-friendly New Tab replacement that offers shortcuts, quick links, a to-do list, custom search engines, local file access, and a live clock — all running locally with no data collection. Your privacy matters, and this policy explains exactly what data the extension handles.

Data Collection
Home Tab does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Specifically:

No analytics or tracking — The extension contains no analytics scripts, telemetry, or tracking pixels.
No remote servers — No data is ever sent to any external server owned or operated by the developer.
No user accounts — The extension does not require or support sign-in of any kind.
No cookies — The extension does not read or write browser cookies.

Local Storage
All user preferences (theme, quick links, shortcuts, search engines, to-dos, and background settings) are stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in chrome.storage.local API. This data:

Never leaves your device.
Is not accessible to the developer or any third party.
Is automatically removed if you uninstall the extension.

Network Requests
The extension makes the following optional network requests, initiated only by your actions:

Request	Purpose	When
suggestqueries.google.com	Search autocomplete suggestions	When you type in the search bar
www.google.com/s2/favicons	Website favicon images	When rendering quick links and engine icons
No personal or identifying information is included in these requests beyond the search query you type.

Permissions
Permission	Reason
history	To show recent browsing history in search suggestions (read-only, never stored or transmitted)
storage	To save your preferences locally on your device
tabs	To open internal browser pages (settings, extensions, etc.) from quick tools

Third-Party Services
Home Tab does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, or data brokers.

Changes to This Policy
If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in this document with an updated date. No retroactive changes will be made that reduce your privacy protections.

Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's repository.
